I have a pocket mod for my vocal warm-up phrases. A pocket mod has 6 (interior) pages, so I picked 18 phrases (I wanted each phrase to show up twice), shuffled them, and then arranged them on each of the pages. Duplicates on a given page were swapped with the first would-be-non-duplicate on the next. The final results were:

On the days when I use these warm-up phrases as part of my vocal exercises, I roll 12 d6s. The first d6 (I put them in arbitrary order) determines the page for that day, and each of the dozen is used to determine which phrase I read from that page. I read each phrase twice, unless consecutive d6s have the same value, in which case I read the phrase twice for the first die, then an extra time for each subsequent (to reduce tedium).

In this way, I am able to randomize my voice training, which saves executive function and serves to introduce variety into my learning.
